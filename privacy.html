Privacy Policy

Compound Daily · Last updated: June 2026

Compound Daily ("we", "our") operates a faceless personal-finance media channel and an in-house automation tool used solely to publish our own original videos to our own social-media accounts. This policy explains what data the tool touches and how we handle it.

Who this applies to

The tool is operated only by the channel owner and authenticates only the owner's own accounts. We do not offer the tool to other users and we do not access, collect, or post on behalf of any third party.

Data we access

  • Authorization tokens. When the owner connects an account (e.g. TikTok via Login Kit / Content Posting API, or YouTube via the YouTube Data API), the platform issues an access token and refresh token. These are stored locally on the owner's own computer only, and are used solely to upload the owner's own videos.
  • Basic account info. We may read basic profile information (e.g. the connected account's identifier and the privacy options the account allows) only to complete a valid upload.
  • Video content. The original videos we generate, plus their titles, descriptions, and captions, are sent to the platform's official publishing API.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use the data for advertising or profiling.
  • We do not collect personal data about viewers or other people.

Storage & retention

Authorization tokens are stored locally (on the owner's machine) for as long as the integration is in use. They are never transmitted to any server other than the official platform APIs (Google/YouTube, TikTok).

Data deletion & revoking access

You can revoke this tool's access at any time from the platform's own settings — for TikTok: TikTok app → Settings → Security & permissions → Manage app permissions; for Google/YouTube: myaccount.google.com/permissions. Deleting the local token file (tiktok_token.json / youtube_token.json) removes our stored credentials. To request deletion of any data, email us at the address below.

Third-party platforms

Our use of Google/YouTube data complies with the Google Privacy Policy and the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Our use of TikTok data complies with TikTok's Developer Terms and policies.
